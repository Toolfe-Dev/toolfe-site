Job Overview

Sales and Marketing professionals are responsible for promoting and selling products or services to customers. They leverage market research, develop effective sales strategies, and engage in customer relationship management to drive revenue growth. Strong communication, negotiation, and analytical skills are essential for success in these roles.

Responsibilities:

  • Support B2B sales and marketing initiatives.
  • Assist in creating marketing materials and presentations.
  • Conduct market research and analyze industry trends.
  • Collaborate in planning and executing B2B marketing campaigns.
  • Communicate with potential business clients.

Requirements :

  • Female candidates only.
  • Recent MBA graduates preferred.
  • Strong interest in B2B sales and marketing.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team.
  • Prior experience or coursework in sales/marketing is a plus.

Benefits :

  • Certification upon completion of the internship.
  • Hands-on experience in B2B sales and marketing.
  • Stipend of ₹5000 for the 3-month duration.
  • Additional incentives for successful intern-driven sales conversion.
