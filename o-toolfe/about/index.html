Today

Toolfe is an IT company that provides businesses with comprehensive, cost-effective technology solutions and consulting.

Our story

At one point of time, we began to realize the intensity of lack of tech adoption has grown too much but ironically it is the technology development that has grown enormously. We also realized many tech companies are focussing on larger market bases and greater project size rather than generating efficient values below the medium scale economies. This gap is mostly filled by freelancers or start ups. However, both entities are technically skilled but they lack the research acumen. This dragged us passionately to work for a purpose that aligns with our inherent trait of helping others diversely in various aspects of information as well as technology they struggle to afford and access for the new age future. We are proud to represent our cause - “Toolfe”, a cute acronym of “information as a TOOL For Economy, Efficiency and Effectiveness”

Our Vision

We amplify & accelerate your purpose with advanced information and technology driving the sustainable society.

Our Mission
