Job Overview

We are seeking a talented and driven B2B Sales Development Representative to join our growing team!

In this role, you will be responsible for driving new business growth through effective B2B sales strategies. You will leverage your strong communication and relationship-building skills to engage with potential clients, understand their needs, and present our solutions effectively.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement B2B sales strategies to penetrate target markets and achieve sales goals.
  • Conduct market research to identify emerging trends and business opportunities.
  • Generate leads and qualify prospects by utilizing various lead generation techniques.
  • Build strong relationships with clients, understand their needs, and present solutions effectively.
  • Manage client relationships through a CRM system, track sales processes, and analyze data.
  • Prepare and deliver persuasive presentations that communicate product value and strategic plans.
  • Negotiate deals, address client concerns, and secure favorable terms.
  • Collaborate with team members to share insights, align strategies, and elevate overall sales performance.
  • Maintain a strong sales pipeline and consistently achieve or exceed sales targets.

Requirements :

  • MBA degree from an accredited institution (preferred)
  • 1-3 years of experience in B2B sales and business development
  • Strong understanding of sales methodologies and client relationship management
  • Proven ability to close sales deals and achieve sales goals
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a focus on building rapport
  • Expertise in MS Office tools (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Practical experience with CRM software
  • Experience working with different markets clients (a plus)
  • Analytical skills for market research and data analysis
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment
